Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your pet's first swipe.

General
TinPets is a pet matchmaking and social app that helps pet owners find compatible companions for their animals. Whether you have a dog, cat, rabbit, or any other pet, TinPets connects you with nearby pet owners whose animals match your pet's personality, energy level, and play style.
Any pet! While dogs and cats are most common, TinPets supports all animal types — rabbits, guinea pigs, birds, reptiles, and more. Our matching algorithm adapts based on species and personality traits specific to each animal type.
TinPets is active in over 120 cities across the US, Canada, UK, and Australia. Even in smaller cities, we have users — and our community is growing daily. You can set your radius from 1 km to 50 km so you never miss a compatible pet nearby.
Our algorithm considers your pet's species, breed, age, energy level, personality tags (playful, calm, shy, social, etc.), and your location. It also learns from past matches — if your pet consistently gets along with certain personality types, we show you more of those. The compatibility percentage you see on each profile is a real score, not decorative.
Account & Privacy
Only your first name and city are shown publicly. Your full address, phone number, and email are never shared. You control when and whether to share location details — we only suggest meeting in public, pet-friendly places.

Pet Safety
We recommend starting with a neutral, public space like a dog park or quiet outdoor area. Keep both pets on leads for the first introduction, let them sniff and approach at their own pace, and keep the first session short (15–20 minutes). TinPets shows you a "First Meet Guide" tailored to your pets' species and personality types once you match.
Not every match works out in person — that's completely normal. You can un-match any time, and we'll use the outcome to improve future suggestions. Our algorithm gets smarter with every interaction.
