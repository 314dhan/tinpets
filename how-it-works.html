How TinPets Works

Three simple steps between your pet and their new best friend.

1

Create Your Pet's Profile

Upload your pet's best photos, add their name, breed, age, and personality traits. The more you share, the better their matches will be. It takes less than two minutes.

  • Add up to 6 photos
  • Set personality tags (playful, calm, energetic…)
  • Set your location radius
