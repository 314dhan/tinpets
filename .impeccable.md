## Design Context

### Users
Pet owners (dog & cat owners primarily) who want to socialize and find companions for their pets. Casual-to-invested pet enthusiasts who enjoy consumer apps. Using this on mobile and desktop.

### Brand Personality
Premium & Polished with warmth. Three words: **refined, warm, confident**.
Elevated without being cold — feels like a curated lifestyle experience, not a generic startup landing page. Think premium consumer app with genuine personality.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Color**: Evolve existing coral (#FF6B6B) + teal (#4ECDC4) palette — deepen and saturate, make them feel premium not generic. Warm the neutrals (tinted grays toward coral). No pure blacks/whites.
- **Typography**: Replace Poppins with something more distinctive for headings. Fredoka One can stay for the logo. Body text stays readable and refined.
- **Motion**: Purposeful, premium animations — scroll-driven reveals, smooth micro-interactions, satisfying hover states. No bounce/elastic. ease-out-quart/expo easing only.
- **Anti-references**: Generic Bootstrap template feel, AI cyan-purple gradients, glassmorphism everywhere, identical card grids.

### Design Principles
1. **Premium warmth** — every surface should feel considered, not templated
2. **Motion as polish** — animations reveal quality, not decoration
3. **Color with intention** — deepen existing brand colors, never add color randomly
4. **Typography as drama** — use scale and weight contrast to create visual hierarchy
5. **Delight without obstruction** — personality in the details, never blocking the user
